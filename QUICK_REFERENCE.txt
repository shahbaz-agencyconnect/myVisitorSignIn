🎯 THE 4 FLOWS (Auto-Detected):
────────────────────────────────
1. EXISTING_ACCOUNT
   └─ User signed in before → Click Yes

2. NEW_SIGN_IN_CONFIRMATION
   └─ User signed in elsewhere → Confirm new session

3. OTP_VERIFICATION
   └─ New user/device → Enter 6-digit OTP

4. CHANGE_DETAILS
   └─ Details changed → Confirm unchanged
